Our Platform

Pirate Program

Our party program focuses on digital sovereignty, transparent governance, and protecting the civil liberties of every citizen in the digital age.

💻

Digital Technology in Our Daily Lives

Digital tools have become an integral part of our lives. We stand for responsible digital transformation that respects privacy and empowers individuals.

Digital Rights Privacy E-Government
🏛️

Data Taxation

Large corporations collect and monetize citizen data. We propose fair taxation frameworks on data exploitation to ensure digital equity.

Fair Tax Data Economy Regulation
🌐

Neutral, Open, and Sovereign Computing

We advocate for open-source software, net neutrality, and computing infrastructure that remains free from corporate and government surveillance.

Open Source Net Neutrality Sovereignty
🔒

Surveillance & Civil Liberties

We oppose mass surveillance and algorithmic policing. Your innocence should not depend on video surveillance at the co-op.

Anti-Surveillance Civil Rights
🤝

Company & Corporate Responsibility

Companies must be held accountable for their digital practices. We push for transparency requirements and ethical technology standards.

Ethics Accountability Standards
📖

Education & Digital Literacy

Every citizen deserves access to digital education. We support programs that teach critical thinking and technological literacy from an early age.

Education Literacy Access
