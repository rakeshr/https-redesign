Our Core Values

What HTTPS
Stands For

Each letter in our name represents a fundamental value that drives our political action and vision for a better society.

Humanisme

Placing human dignity and well-being at the center of every political decision we make.

Transparence

Full transparency in governance, open data, and accountability for all public institutions.

Technologie

Leveraging technology for the common good while protecting digital rights and privacy.

Participation

Direct democracy, citizen engagement, and inclusive decision-making processes.

Solidarité

Standing together for social justice, equal opportunities, and a fair digital future.

Our Vision

Pirate Program

Our comprehensive political program addresses the digital age challenges facing our society today.

Digital Technology in Our Daily Lives

Digital tools have become an integral part of our lives. We advocate for the use of free and open source software to protect citizens and ensure digital sovereignty.

Read more

Data Taxation

We are setting principles to tax data harvesting, ensuring fair returns for citizens whose data is being monetized by large corporations.

Read more

Neutral, Open, and Sovereign Computing

We fight for a digital infrastructure that is neutral, transparent, and under democratic control — not monopolized by Big Tech companies.
